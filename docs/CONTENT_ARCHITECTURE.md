# Crimson Signal Content Architecture v1

## Purpose

This document defines the structure of the Crimson Signal knowledge base.

The goal is to build a scalable research platform, not a traditional marketing website.

Every page should exist within a connected knowledge graph that helps visitors move from a business problem to an informed technology decision.

# Content Types

Crimson Signal uses twelve core content types.

## 1. Industry Hub

Purpose

Provide a complete overview of technology within a specific industry.

Examples

- Restaurants
- Retail
- Healthcare
- Financial Services
- Automotive
- Manufacturing
- Franchise

Every Industry Hub links to:

- Business Problems
- Technologies
- Tools
- Research
- Comparisons
- Buying Guides

---

## 2. Technology Decision Guide

Purpose

Help readers decide whether a technology is worth evaluating.

Examples

- SD-WAN
- Managed Network
- UCaaS
- CCaaS
- Microsoft Teams
- POTS Replacement
- LTE Backup
- Starlink
- SASE

Every Technology Guide links to:

- Related Industries
- Related Business Problems
- Decision Tools
- Comparisons
- Research

---

## 3. Business Problem

Purpose

Explain a business problem before discussing technology.

Examples

- Internet Outages
- POS Downtime
- Vendor Sprawl
- PCI Compliance
- Store Openings
- Network Visibility
- Legacy Phone Lines
- Slow Store Deployments

Every Problem Page links to:

- Technologies
- Industry Pages
- Calculators
- Assessments
- Research

---

## 4. Decision Tool

Purpose

Help visitors quantify or evaluate a decision.

Examples

- Downtime Cost Calculator
- Bandwidth Calculator
- Vendor Consolidation Calculator
- POTS Savings Calculator
- Network Assessment

---

## 5. Comparison Guide

Purpose

Compare competing technologies.

Examples

- SD-WAN vs MPLS
- Teams vs RingCentral
- Fiber vs Broadband
- LTE vs Starlink
- Managed Network vs Internal IT

---

## 6. Checklist

Purpose

Provide actionable implementation guidance.

Examples

- Restaurant Opening Checklist
- Network Audit Checklist
- POTS Inventory Checklist
- Vendor Evaluation Checklist

---

## 7. Assessment

Purpose

Allow visitors to evaluate their current maturity.

Examples

- Network Maturity Assessment
- Technology Readiness Assessment
- Business Continuity Assessment

---

## 8. Research Report

Purpose

Summarize independent market research.

Examples

- Restaurant Technology Trends
- Retail Connectivity Report
- Franchise Technology Benchmark

---

## 9. Case Study Library

Purpose

Summarize public case studies.

Never present vendor marketing.

Instead identify:

- Business problem
- Technology deployed
- Outcome
- Lessons learned

---

## 10. Vendor Profile

Purpose

Provide objective information.

Include:

Overview

Strengths

Tradeoffs

Ideal Customers

Products

Public Case Studies

Related Technologies

Competitors

Never rank vendors.

Never recommend vendors.

---

## 11. Glossary

Purpose

Explain technical terminology using plain English.

---

## 12. Resource

Purpose

Provide downloadable assets.

Examples

Templates

RFPs

Worksheets

PDF Guides

Three Related Guides

Two Related Problems

Two Related Technologies

# Homepage Experience

Visitors should begin by answering one question.

What are you trying to figure out today?

Research an Industry

Evaluate a Technology

Solve a Business Problem

Use a Tool

Explore Research

Compare Technologies

The homepage should feel like entering a research library, not a vendor website.

# Long-Term Goal

Crimson Signal should become the most trusted independent research platform for multi-location business technology decisions.

Every new page should strengthen the knowledge graph instead of simply increasing page count.
