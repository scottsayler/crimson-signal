# Crimson Signal SEO System v1

## Purpose

Crimson Signal is built around topical authority, entity relationships, and search intent.

The objective is not to rank individual pages.

The objective is to become the most trusted source for technology decisions made by multi-location businesses.

Every page should strengthen the overall knowledge graph.

---

# SEO Philosophy

We optimize for people first.

Search engines second.

If a page helps someone make a better decision, it will naturally perform better over time.

Avoid writing for keywords.

Write to answer questions.

40 to 80 words.

# URL Philosophy

Keep URLs short.

Use lowercase.

Use hyphens.

Never include dates.

Never include version numbers.

Good

/industries/restaurants/networking/

Bad

/restaurants-networking-guide-2026/

# Content Freshness

Review cornerstone pages every six months.

Review research pages quarterly.

Review vendor profiles after major product announcements.

Review comparison pages whenever significant market changes occur.

Do not change publication dates unless the content has been meaningfully updated.
