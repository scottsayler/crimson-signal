## Technology context for hospitality

Hospitality organizations manage technology across hotels, resorts, restaurants, and event venues — each property a distinct operating environment with shared brand expectations.

### Guest experience as technology outcome

Wi-Fi quality, mobile check-in, room controls, and payment systems directly affect guest satisfaction scores and review ratings. Technology failures are immediately visible to customers.

### Common technology pressures

- **Property-level connectivity** with varying infrastructure ages and ownership models.
- **Brand standardization** across franchised and managed properties.
- **Operational systems** for reservations, housekeeping, and food service.
- **Seasonal demand** requiring elastic technology capacity.

### How business events affect hospitality

Opening new properties, customer complaints, and outages are the most common triggers for technology strategy work in hospitality.
