## Technology context for financial services

Financial services organizations operate under intense regulatory scrutiny while competing on digital experience. Technology architecture must satisfy auditors, protect customer data, and enable innovation simultaneously.

### Branch and digital convergence

Customers expect seamless experiences whether they visit a branch, use a mobile app, or call a contact center. Technology fragmentation across channels creates both compliance risk and customer friction.

### Common technology pressures

- **Regulatory compliance** across jurisdictions and entity structures.
- **Fraud detection and prevention** at scale across channels.
- **Core system modernization** without disrupting customer-facing operations.
- **Data governance** for AI and analytics initiatives.

### How business events affect financial services

Security requirements, acquisitions, and AI initiatives are the primary drivers of technology strategy conversations in this sector.
