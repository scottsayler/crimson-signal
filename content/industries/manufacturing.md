## Technology context for manufacturing

Manufacturing organizations bridge operational technology on the plant floor with enterprise IT systems. Technology decisions affect production continuity, supply chain visibility, and worker safety.

### OT and IT convergence

The boundary between operational technology and information technology is dissolving. Security, data, and cloud strategies must account for both environments without compromising production systems.

### Common technology pressures

- **Legacy OT systems** with long replacement cycles and limited security updates.
- **Supply chain visibility** requiring data integration across facilities and partners.
- **Predictive maintenance** and AI initiatives dependent on sensor data quality.
- **Multi-site coordination** for production planning and quality management.

### How business events affect manufacturing

AI initiatives, acquisitions, and security requirements are reshaping technology priorities across the manufacturing sector.
