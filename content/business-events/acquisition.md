## Technology implications of M&A

Acquisitions create immediate technology debt. Two organizations means two of everything — often with incompatible architectures, contracts, and security postures.

### What changes

- **Identity fragmentation** creates security risk and user friction from day one.
- **Duplicate infrastructure** inflates costs before synergies materialize.
- **Data integration** complexity is routinely underestimated in deal models.
- **Cultural technology differences** slow decision-making at the worst possible time.

### Integration archetypes

Most organizations choose between rapid consolidation, deliberate coexistence, or selective best-of-breed. Each has different technology implications and timelines.

### Strategic questions

- What is your technology integration thesis?
- Which systems must be unified in year one versus year three?
- How do you maintain operational continuity during transition?
