## Technology implications of leadership change

Leadership transitions reset technology priorities whether the organization is ready or not. New executives inherit architecture decisions, vendor relationships, and technical debt they did not create — and they move quickly to establish direction.

### What changes

- **Decision rights** shift as new leaders question existing technology ownership and governance.
- **Investment priorities** get re-evaluated — projects in flight may be paused, accelerated, or cancelled.
- **Vendor relationships** face scrutiny as new leadership assesses value and alignment.
- **Organizational alignment** between business and technology teams must be re-established.

### Strategic questions

- What technology decisions are locked in versus still open?
- Who owns the technology narrative for the new leadership team?
- Which commitments from the prior regime need explicit review?
