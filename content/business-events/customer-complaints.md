## Technology implications of customer complaints

Customer complaints are often symptoms of technology failures that leadership does not see. Connectivity drops, system latency, and data inconsistencies at individual locations aggregate into brand-level problems.

### What changes

- **Diagnostic capability** becomes essential — you cannot fix what you cannot measure.
- **Location-level monitoring** replaces assumptions about uniform performance.
- **Technology investments** must be evaluated against customer experience outcomes, not just uptime metrics.
- **Cross-functional response** requires IT, operations, and customer teams to share visibility.

### Strategic questions

- Can you correlate complaints to specific locations and systems?
- Is your technology stack designed for consistent customer experience?
- What would your customers say about your digital and physical touchpoints?
